Tutdocs - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

1. Overview

Welcome to Tutdocs ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, and disclose personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect various types of personal information from users, including but not limited to:

  • Profile picture
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Address
  • University or college affiliation
  • Content uploaded for posts

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of facilitating the sharing of study materials among Tutdocs users. Your personal information, including uploaded content, may be shared with other users on the platform for this purpose.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with third parties only to the extent necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or protect our rights.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no security measures are foolproof.

6. Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of Tutdocs.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any material changes.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@tutdocs.com.

9. Legal Compliance

Tutdocs operates in compliance with applicable privacy laws. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
